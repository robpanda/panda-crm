# Panda CRM - Call Center Rep Quick Start Guide

## 1. Logging In

1. Go to **crm.pandaexteriors.com** (or the CRM URL provided)
2. Enter your email address (e.g., `yourname@pandaexteriors.com`)
3. Enter your temporary password: `Panda2025!`
4. Click **Sign In**

> **First-time login:** You may be asked to change your password. Create a strong password with at least 8 characters.

---

## 2. Home Dashboard

When you log in, you'll see your **Call Center Dashboard** with:

### Your Stats
- **My Leads Created** - Leads you've entered today/this week/this month
- **Appointments Set** - Appointments you've scheduled
- **Conversion Rate** - Your lead-to-appointment percentage
- **Team Total** - All leads created by the call center team

### Time Period Toggle
Click **Today**, **This Week**, or **This Month** to change the stats view.

### Leaderboard
See where you rank compared to your teammates! The leaderboard shows:
- Lead count for each rep
- Your current rank position
- Manager view (if you're a manager) shows all team members

---

## 3. Leads Dashboard

Click **Leads** in the top navigation, then **Lead Dashboard**.

### What You'll See:
- **My Performance** - Your personal lead stats
- **Filter tabs**: All Leads, New, Contacted, Qualified, My Leads
- **Search bar** - Find leads by name or phone

### Quick Actions:
- **+ New Lead** - Create a new lead (top right)
- **View All Leads** - See the full leads list

---

## 4. Leads List

Click **Leads** → **Lead List** to see all leads.

### List Features:
- **Search** - Type a name or phone number
- **Filter by Status** - Use tabs to filter (New, Contacted, Qualified)
- **Sort** - Click column headers to sort
- **Pagination** - Navigate between pages

### Lead Card Shows:
- Customer name and phone
- Lead status (color-coded badge)
- Days since created
- Owner (who created/owns the lead)

---

## 5. Creating a New Lead

### Method 1: Quick Add
1. Click **+ New Lead** button (top right of any Leads page)
2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **First Name** & **Last Name**
   - **Phone Number**
   - **Email** (optional but recommended)
   - **Address** (street, city, state, zip)
   - **Lead Source** (how they found us)
3. Click **Create Lead**

### Method 2: Lead Wizard
1. Click **Leads** → **New Lead**
2. Follow the step-by-step wizard:
   - **Step 1:** Basic Info (name, contact)
   - **Step 2:** Address Details
   - **Step 3:** Lead Source & Notes
   - **Step 4:** Set Appointment (optional)
3. Click **Create Lead** when done

### Required Fields:
- First Name
- Last Name
- Phone Number

### Optional But Helpful:
- Email address
- Full address (for territory assignment)
- Lead source (referral, web, canvass, etc.)
- Notes about the call

---

## 6. Setting an Appointment

When creating or editing a lead:

1. Check the **Set Appointment** option
2. Select a **Date** from the calendar
3. Choose a **Time Slot**
4. Add any **Appointment Notes**
5. The system will assign the best available sales rep based on:
   - Territory (by zip code)
   - Skills/certifications
   - Availability

## 8. Tips for Success

1. **Enter complete addresses** - This helps route leads to the right sales rep
2. **Add notes** - Include call details, customer concerns, property info
3. **Set follow-up reminders** - Keep track of callbacks needed
4. **Check your stats** - Track your progress throughout the day
5. **Use the leaderboard** - A little competition keeps everyone motivated!
